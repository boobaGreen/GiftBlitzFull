# 🎬 GiftBlitz — PLAN B: The Cinematic Cut (Ironic Edition)

> **Tone**: Epic movie trailer meets dry British humor — clever, witty, never vulgar
> **Style**: Blockbuster film trailer that gradually reveals it's a hackathon project by one person
> **The Joke**: Treating the gift card problem like a global crisis worthy of a Hollywood film
> **Tool**: Nano Banana Video (AI clips) + Screen Recording + CapCut
> **Duration**: 5:00 max

---

## 🎭 The Core Comedy Rule

The irony works on **contrast**:
- **Epic visuals + dramatic music** → for a problem about... gift cards
- **Hollywood narration** → for a dApp made by one guy
- **Game theory explained like a heist movie** → it's actually just math
- **The "reveal" at the end** → one developer, unlimited espresso

**Never wink at the camera too hard.** Play it straight — the humor comes from HOW seriously you treat something mundane. Think Wes Anderson, not slapstick.
