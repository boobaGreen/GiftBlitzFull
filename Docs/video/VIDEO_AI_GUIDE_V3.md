# 🎬 GiftBlitz — Official Video Production Guide

> **Goal**: A clear 4–5 minute walkthrough for the MasterZ × IOTA Hackathon jury.
> **Language**: Voiceover in **English** (required for the hackathon).
> **Format**: High-quality screen recording + live product demonstration.

---

## 📋 PRE-RECORDING CHECKLIST

1.  **Tab A (SELLER)**: IOTA wallet connected (Address 1), holding 50+ IOTA Testnet.
2.  **Tab B (BUYER)**: IOTA wallet connected (Address 2), holding 50+ IOTA Testnet.
3.  **Environment**: 1920x1080 resolution, clean browser (no bookmarks bar), "Do Not Disturb" on.
4.  **Audio**: Use a dedicated mic if possible. Speak at a steady, confident pace.

---

## 🎬 STEP-BY-STEP RECORDING GUIDE

### PART 1: INTRO (0:00 – 0:30)

- **🎥 VIDEO**: Start on the **GiftBlitz Homepage**. Slowly scroll through the hero section and features to show the dApp is live and functional.
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > _"Hi, I'm Claudio Dall'Ara. Every year, billions of dollars in gift cards go unused or are lost to high-fee middlemen and scams. GiftBlitz is a decentralized exchange on IOTA that uses smart contracts and game theory to create a safe, trustless marketplace. We've removed the intermediaries to give the value back to the users. Let's see how it works in practice."_

---

### PART 2: THE SELLER FLOW (0:30 – 1:50)

- **🎥 VIDEO**: **(Tab A - Seller)**
  1. Click "Connect Wallet" (confirm popup).
  2. **NEW STEP**: Show the **"Setup Your Passport"** screen. Click **"Initialize Passport ID"** and sign the transaction.
  3. Navigate to the **"Create"** page.
  4. Fill the form: Brand: **Nintendo** | Face Value: **10 IOTA** | Price: **8 IOTA**.
  5. Type a dummy gift card code and click **"Create"** (sign transaction).
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > _"As a seller, I connect my wallet. Before my first trade, I initialize my **Citizen Passport**. This is a non-transferable on-chain identity that enables the **reputation-based security** to protect our trades. Once the passport is ready, I list my gift card._
  >
  > _The system handles the security for me—automatically protecting the code and locking a deposit equal to the card’s value._
  >
  > _This 'skin in the game' ensures that I have every reason to be honest, as any attempt to cheat would result in a mathematical loss for me. It’s security through logic, not just words."_

---

### PART 3: THE BUYER FLOW (1:50 – 2:50)

- **🎥 VIDEO**: **(Tab B - Buyer)**
  1. Refresh the **"Market"** page.
  2. Find the **Nintendo** box you just created and click it.
  3. On the purchase page, click to **Buy and Initialize Identity** (single action).
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > _"Switching to the buyer: I find a great deal on a Nintendo card.
  > When I buy it, the system automatically initializes my identity and locks a small trust deposit.
  > This double-lock mechanism means both parties have something at stake. On GiftBlitz, honesty is the only way for everyone to get their funds back, making fraud simply irrational."_

---

### PART 4: REVEAL & FINALIZE (2:50 – 3:50)

- **🎥 VIDEO**:
  1. **(Tab A - Seller)**: Go to Profile -> My Trades. Click **"Reveal Key"** and sign the TX.
  2. **(Tab B - Buyer)**: Refresh the trade page. Click **"Finalize"** and sign the TX.
  3. Show the final **"Trade Completed"** success message.
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > \*"Once the trade is locked, the seller reveals the code.
  >
  > The buyer verifies it's working, clicks 'Finalize', and the trade settles instantly.
  >
  > The seller gets paid, and both of our deposits are returned automatically. No middlemen, no waiting days for approval—just a smooth, secure transaction."\*

---

### PART 5: REPUTATION & TECH (3:50 – 4:45)

- **🎥 VIDEO**:
  1. Navigate to the **Profile** page.
  2. Highlight the updated **Citizen Passport / Reputation NFT**. Show that trade volume and stats have increased.
  3. Briefly show the **IOTA Explorer** with the verified Move package.
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > _"Every successful trade also builds my on-chain reputation. This identity tracks my history and rewards long-term honesty by unlocking higher trade limits over time. Everything is powered by IOTA, ensuring the entire process is transparent, decentralized, and faster than any traditional alternative."_

---

### PART 6: OUTRO (4:45 – 5:00)

- **🎥 VIDEO**: Return to the **Homepage** and show the final links/socials.
- **🎙️ AUDIO (Script)**:
  > _"GiftBlitz is the fair, fast, and secure way to unlock the value in your gift cards. Thanks for watching. I'm Claudio Dall'Ara, and this is GiftBlitz."_
