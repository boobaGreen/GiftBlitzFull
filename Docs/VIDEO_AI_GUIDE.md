# 🍌 GiftBlitz — AI Video Production Guide (Nano Banana Video)

> **Tool**: Nano Banana Video (https://nanobananavideo.com)
> **Strategy**: Use AI for cinematic intro, transitions & outro + real screen recording for the demo

---

## ⚠️ Important Limitation

Nano Banana generates clips of **5-10 seconds max**. It creates cinematic/artistic footage — **NOT** screen recordings.

**For a hackathon, judges MUST see the real product working.** So the strategy is:

| Part | What to use | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Intro (0:00–0:30) | 🍌 **Banana AI clips** + voiceover or text | Cinematic first impression |
| Transitions | 🍌 **Banana AI clips** (5s each) | Professional look between sections |
| **Live Demo (0:30–3:30)** | 📹 **Real screen recording** | **Judges need to see the real dApp** |
| Closing (3:30–5:00) | 🍌 **Banana AI clips** + voiceover or text | Strong ending |

---
